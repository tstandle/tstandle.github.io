Calculating Pupularity

In this project, my team and I set out to conduct a study on dog-themed Twitter accounts to see if we could identify any potential correlations between aspects of the account tweets and user engagement. We selected two popular dog-themed twitter accounts, WeRateDogs (@dog_rates) and Thoughts of Dog (@dog_feelings) to collect data from using python scripts to extract all of the tweets from the two accounts’ timelines. We then used custom scripts to extract and analyze specific features and entities of the tweets to calculate the correlations between the features and user engagement to see if we could identify any high correlations.

Study Report

The provided report recounts the full details of our study and the results of our analysis in addition to future steps that my team and I would like to take with our investigation.


Sample Code - Top Terms

A sample display of one of the custom scripts that I created with my team to aid in our study. This specific script was focused on parsing through the two timelines and identifying the top terms that occurred within the accounts. The script took into account a custom list of stopwords in order to ensure that the most accurate, and contextually relevant terms for the accounts were included.
