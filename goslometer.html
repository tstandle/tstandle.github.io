"The Goslometer"

With this paper prototype, I had the task of designing for a mobile app that allows users to track the location and quantities of goslings that a user sees throughout the day. The goal of the app is to help users locate goslings and report a siting so that others may find and spot the baby geese as well.

Design Specifications

The prototype was required to support the following user activities:

  • Log in and/or register.
  • Enter and edit the information about locations.
  • Enter information and upload pictures about the specific goslings.
  • Display the gosling spotting locations in comparison to one another (e.g., distance between them, type of location (park, fountain, lake, etc.).
